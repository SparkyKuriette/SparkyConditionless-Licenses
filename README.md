# The SparkyConditionless Licences.
## Information (this is the preamble).
Many licences have various restrictions, like attribution and recursivity (copyleft, share-alike, et cetera). These licences don't have those restrictions, instead making works under these licences usable without restrictions (unless where required by law), whilst retaining the respective intellectual property, unlike Creative Commons Zero and the Unlicense, and like ISC-0, BSD-0 and MIT-0. 3 out of 4 of these licences also have patent and/or trademark grants, allowing patents and trademarks to be licenced.
